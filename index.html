The Wagah-Attari border is a fascinating and atmospheric place to visit and hearing the roar of the Indian people dwarf the Pakistanis makes me feel proud to be an Indian.

Harmandir Sahib is a very charming and religious place to visit for Sikhs and is an experience every person should be exposed to at least once in their lifetime.

The Sydney Opera House is a marvellous piece of architecture close to the heart of australians and is famously reknown worldwide.

Australia 108 is a skyscraper situated in Melbourne City and has taken the title as the tallest skyscraper in the Southern Hemisphere outdoing the Eureka Skydeck and Q1 (In Melbourne and Gold Coast respectively) at a staggering 323 meters to the tip.
